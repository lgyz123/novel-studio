# Scene Contract Patterns

Use the smallest pattern that fits the task.

## Discovery scene

- New information must be verifiable
- The protagonist's understanding changes
- Ending leaves a new action vector or new fear

## Decision scene

- The protagonist chooses between at least two paths
- The choice changes later handling, not only emotion
- Ending makes the new posture visible

## Consequence scene

- Earlier action produces a concrete cost or complication
- The scene cannot return to pre-consequence normal
- Ending confirms the new pressure

## External resistance scene

- Resistance comes from environment, institution, another person, or surveillance
- It interrupts an intended action
- Ending either blocks, redirects, or raises the cost of trying again

## Bridge scene

- Only valid if it preserves chapter momentum
- Must still land at least one of:
  - new information
  - new action
  - changed state
  - visible consequence
