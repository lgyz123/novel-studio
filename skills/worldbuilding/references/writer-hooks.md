# Writer Hooks

Convert setting into things a scene can actually use.

## Good hooks

- a job that exposes hierarchy
- a place where cost becomes visible
- a routine that reveals institutional pressure
- a transaction that shows unequal power
- a material trace left by danger or regulation

## Weak hooks

- abstract historical summary with no present trace
- lore that changes nothing about behavior
- terminology with no consequence

## Rule of thumb

If a setting patch cannot plausibly affect:

- labor
- movement
- concealment
- negotiation
- punishment
- or survival choices

then it is probably too abstract for writer use.
