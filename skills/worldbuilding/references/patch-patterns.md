# Worldbuilding Patch Patterns

## Institutional patch

Use when the world has ideas but no visible handling chain.

Questions:

- Which institution touches this problem first?
- Which people do the dangerous work?
- How is the cost hidden, taxed, or normalized?

## Regional patch

Use when the world has themes but no stage depth.

Questions:

- What are the main places where pressure becomes visible?
- How do goods, people, and danger move through those places?
- What differs from one area to another?

## Power-system patch

Use when the world mentions a force system but not its lived cost.

Questions:

- Who can access it?
- What does it cost?
- What is forbidden, unstable, or socially punished?
- How does a non-practitioner get hurt by it indirectly?
