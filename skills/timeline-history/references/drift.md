# Timeline Drift

## Same-night blur

Pattern:

- prior scene ends at night
- new scene is next morning but still reads like the same night

Fix:

- mark the time early
- give one sensory sign of the changed hour

## History overload

Pattern:

- timeline note turns into an isolated history lecture

Fix:

- keep only the anchor that explains present pressure

## Missing relative order

Pattern:

- reader cannot tell whether the new scene is hours, a day, or longer after the last one

Fix:

- state the gap or imply it with labor, light, and physical carry-over
